About

Building smarter campuses for a sustainable future, cam empowers educational institutions to gain full visibility into their energy consumption. Through real-time monitoring and data-driven insights, the platform enables administrators, finance teams, and sustainability officers to simultaneously cut energy costs by 25% and reduce carbon footprints by 40%.

_Roles

System Administrator

  • Monitors and maintains the energy management system.
  • Manages system configuration and data setup.
  • Tracks energy performance and analytics.
  • Ensures smooth system operation.

Financial Analyst

  • Calculates utility costs and financial metrics.
  • Generates reports (PDF/CSV).
  • Evaluates financial impact of energy projects.
  • Performs trend analysis for investment decisions.

Technician

  • Performs technical maintenance of energy systems.
  • Responds to real-time alerts.
  • Diagnoses equipment issues.
  • Ensures reliable operation.

Sustainability Officer

  • Plans sustainability initiatives.
  • Monitors environmental impact.
  • Prepares sustainability reports.
  • Ensures compliance with standards.
