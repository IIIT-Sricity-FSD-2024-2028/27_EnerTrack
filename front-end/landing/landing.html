The Mission

Building smarter campuses for a sustainable future.

EnerTrack empowers educational institutions to gain full visibility into their energy consumption. Through real-time monitoring and data-driven insights, our platform identifies inefficiencies before they become costs.

By connecting administrators, finance teams, and sustainability officers, EnerTrack has helped institutions cut energy costs by an average of 25% and reduce carbon footprints by 40%.

Stakeholders

Tailored for every project lead.

System Administrator

  • Monitors and maintains energy management infrastructure.
  • Manages system configuration and sensor data setup.
  • Tracks real-time system performance and analytics.
  • Ensures 24/7 mission-critical operations.

Financial Analyst

  • Calculates utility costs and financial ROI metrics.
  • Generates precise budgetary reports (PDF/CSV).
  • Evaluates financial impact of energy projects.
  • Predictive trend analysis for investment decisions.

Technician

  • Executes technical maintenance and sensor calibration.
  • Responds instantly to real-time anomaly alerts.
  • Diagnoses equipment issues with FDD tools.
  • Maintains hardware integrity across campus.

Sustainability Officer

  • Strategy lead for campus-wide green initiatives.
  • Monitors long-term environmental target compliance.
  • Prepares global sustainability impact reports.
  • Drives behavioral change through data visibility.
Core Capabilities

Real-time Insights. Automated Alerts. Sustainable Data.
